Our History

Founded in 2015, Football Agency Sierra Leone has been at the forefront of developing African football talent. We started with a vision to bridge the gap between African players and global opportunities, creating pathways for young athletes to reach their full potential on the international stage.

Our Mission

Our mission is to discover, develop, and represent the finest football talent from Sierra Leone and across Africa. We are committed to providing comprehensive support, from grassroots development to professional representation, ensuring our players receive the guidance and opportunities they deserve to succeed at the highest levels of the game.
